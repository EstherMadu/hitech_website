Environmental, Social, Health, and Safety Excellence

Our Environment, Social, Health, and Safety Strategy reflects our unwavering dedication to delivering exceptional sustainability performance in every aspect of our projects. From the outset, we prioritize the integration of international environmental and social standards, aligning ourselves with the stringent requirements set by the International Finance Corporation (IFC) and the World Bank, especially in internationally financed projects. We understand the importance of navigating diverse legal frameworks, and our comprehensive Environmental and Social Management System (ESMS) ensures seamless compliance while exceeding international performance standards.

Strategic Environmental & Social Objectives

The Strategic Environmental & Social (E&S) Objectives set forth by HITECH are designed to guide the organization towards sustainable and responsible operations, ensuring compliance and enhancing the positive impact on communities and the environment. The Strategic E&S Objectives are presented below:

  1. Compliance with Legal and Regulatory Requirements
  2. Sustainable Resource Management
  3. Pollution Prevention
  4. Biodiversity Conservation
  5. Community Engagement and Development
  6. Health and Safety
  7. Economic and Social Benefits
  8. Continuous Improvement
  9. Capacity Building
  10. Climate Change Mitigation and Adaptation

Environmental Commitment

  • Compliance: We adhere to standard environmental practices and comply with all relevant regulations.
  • Resource Management: We optimize natural resource consumption, practice efficient energy management, minimize waste, and actively engage in recycling.
  • Waste Disposal: We employ government-certified waste disposal companies for environmentally safe waste management.
  • Emergency Response: We maintain effective procedures for prompt response, spillage control, and cleanup.
  • Ecosystem Preservation: We minimize adverse effects on air, water, soil, and biodiversity, and replant native species in disturbed areas.
  • Employee Awareness: We prioritize sustainability awareness and environmental management in employee training.
  • Collaboration: We consult with relevant authorities and specialists to seek advice and achieve environmental management goals.
  • Continuous Improvement: We conduct regular audits and assessments to ensure compliance and strive for ongoing improvement.

Health and Safety

  • Leadership and Commitment: We demonstrate resilient leadership and commitment to health and safety, setting objectives and maintaining high standards.
  • Sanitary Facilities: We provide adequate sanitary facilities on all construction sites, ensuring hygiene and accessibility.
  • Awareness and Culture: We foster a culture where health and safety are integrated into every aspect of our operations.
  • Competence and Training: We provide comprehensive training and supervision, equipping employees with necessary knowledge and skills.
  • Stakeholder Safety: We prioritize the health and safety of clients, employees, the public, visitors, and subcontractors.
  • Effective Management System: We maintain an efficient health and safety management system, exceeding national and international regulations.

Grievance Redress Mechanism

HITECH has established a Grievance Redress Mechanism (GRM) to ensure that all complaints or concerns, whether from workers, communities, or external stakeholders, are addressed promptly, fairly, and transparently. The GRM supports our commitment to the IFC Performance Standards, Equator Principles, and Nigerian laws by offering accessible, confidential, and non-retaliatory channels for lodging project-related concerns.

Who Can Use the GRM?

  • Local community members
  • Workers and subcontractors
  • Civil society groups
  • Concerned individuals or stakeholders

How to Submit a Grievance:

Grievance forms available at company offices and project sites

Email: grievances@hitech-company.com

Phone or SMS: +234 902 969 2156

In-person: with our Site Admins and Community Liaison Officers (CLOs)

Anonymous complaints are accepted and processed through grievance boxes.

Worker-Specific Grievances:

A parallel system exists for employment-related issues like pay disputes, harassment, or safety concerns managed through the HR department and aligned with our Human Resources Policy and Code of Conduct. We disclose GRM performance data in ESG reports and stakeholder meetings, to build trust and accountability.
