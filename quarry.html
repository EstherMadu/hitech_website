Hitech Quarry

The granite rock deposits within our quarry are distinguished by their high specific gravity and solidity compared to those in the southeastern region of Nigeria. These areas are part of the crystalline rocks of the Nigeria Pre-Cambrian Basement complex. The granitic rock throughout our quarry area forms massive outcroppings at higher elevations above sea level.

Commitment to Best Practices and Environmental Standards

At Hitech Construction Co. Ltd, we take pride in using the industry's best methods for blasting and aligning with all international standards in waste management, stakeholder engagement, and all other environmental and social requirements necessary for a quarry operation. We adhere to the Equator Principles and IFC Performance Standards, ensuring responsible and sustainable quarrying practices.
