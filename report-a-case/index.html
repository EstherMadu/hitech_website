How review works

A calmer path from issue to action.

  1. 1 Capture the facts. Focus on what happened, where it happened, and what is known right now.
  2. 2 Clarify the risk. Note whether the matter affects safety, compliance, people, delivery, or the surrounding community.
  3. 3 Route it correctly. Use the direct links below when the matter should go through a broader compliance or contact path.

What makes a strong case report?

  • Use specific locations, teams, dates, and visible facts.
  • Separate observed events from assumptions or conclusions.
  • State whether the issue is ongoing, resolved, or still escalating.
  • Highlight any urgent safety or environmental risk clearly.
