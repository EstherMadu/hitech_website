Foam Bitumen Stabilised Materials: Revolutionizing Construction

At Hitech, we are at the forefront of the Foam Bitumen Stabilised Materials revolution. This innovative method blends bitumen with foam to create a stable, durable, and environmentally conscious material for a wide range of construction applications.

From road construction to earthworks, the technique delivers standout benefits in strength, cost-effectiveness, and sustainability, helping clients build longer-lasting infrastructure with better control over resources.

  • Improves structural performance while supporting more efficient material use.
  • Creates a durable base for demanding transport and civil infrastructure corridors.
  • Supports practical, sustainable delivery strategies for modern project environments.

Applications of Foam Bitumen Stabilised Materials

Road Construction

Road building and rehabilitation with longer-term durability

Our expertise in Foam Bitumen Stabilised Materials supports road construction and rehabilitation projects that remain durable and dependable, even in challenging environments.

Earthworks

Stable embankments and stronger working platforms

This technique proves invaluable for earthworks and embankments, creating solid foundations that improve stability and support a wide range of civil structures.

Remediation

Sustainable environmental remediation solutions

Foam Bitumen Stabilised Materials are a strong fit for remediation work, helping provide containment, control, and dependable long-term ground performance.
